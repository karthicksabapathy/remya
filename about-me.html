About me

THe story of the artist
I am Remya Thottingal, a painting artist whose creative journey has been fueled by a lifelong passion for art. From a young age, I found joy in expressing myself through colors and shapes, a joy that has only deepened over the years. My work is characterized by its vibrant palette and dynamic compositions, often drawing inspiration from the natural world and human emotions. Each piece I create is an exploration of mood, atmosphere, and storytelling, inviting viewers to see the world through my eyes.
My dedication to my craft has led to numerous exhibitions and accolades, solidifying my reputation as a talented and innovative artist. Beyond the canvas, I am committed to sharing my knowledge and inspiring others, conducting workshops, and engaging with the art community. Whether through my detailed portraits or expansive landscapes, my art continues to captivate and inspire those who encounter it.
